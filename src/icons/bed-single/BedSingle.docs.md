# BedSingle Icon

Dynamic dynamic animated asset part of the Universal Motion Engine powered by Ollama AI.

## AI Philosophy
> The bed-single icon is designed to have a soft, inviting feel with subtle animations that mimic the comfort and relaxation of sleeping in a single bed. The idle animation creates a slight rotation and scale increase to represent the natural movement and warmth associated with being on a bed. The hover animation adds more motion to simulate the gentle sway one might experience when lying down or looking at the icon from an angle. The click animation includes a vibration effect for haptic feedback, enhancing user interaction.
