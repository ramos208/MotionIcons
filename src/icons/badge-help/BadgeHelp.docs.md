# BadgeHelp Icon

Dynamic dynamic animated asset part of the Universal Motion Engine powered by Ollama AI.

## AI Philosophy
> The motion profile for the badge-help icon is designed to be visually engaging and reflective of its 'help' theme. The idle state drifts subtly, creating a sense of calm and readiness. Upon hovering, there's a momentary burst of energy with a pop effect, which represents alertness or immediate action. When clicked, both a vibration and ripple are implemented to enhance interactivity.
