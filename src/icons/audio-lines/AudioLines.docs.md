# AudioLines Icon

Dynamic dynamic animated asset part of the Universal Motion Engine powered by Ollama AI.

## AI Philosophy
> The motion of the audio-lines icon is designed to mimic the sound waves and transitions through a subtle yet dynamic pop effect. The idle animation gently rotates and scales the icon, while the hover adds a playful spin and skew offsetting it from its center. Upon clicking, a subtle vibration effect combined with an expanding ripple effect enhances the interactivity of the icon.
