# BellRing Icon

Dynamic dynamic animated asset part of the Universal Motion Engine powered by Ollama AI.

## AI Philosophy
> The motion design aims to mimic the physical behavior of a bell ringing, with a pendulum-like motion for idle and hover states, emphasizing the vibration effect for click events through sound waves and neon plasma shader.
