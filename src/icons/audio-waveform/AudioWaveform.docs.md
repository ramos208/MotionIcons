# AudioWaveform Icon

Dynamic dynamic animated asset part of the Universal Motion Engine powered by Ollama AI.

## AI Philosophy
> The icon's waveform motion is designed to look both mechanical and elastic, simulating the sound waves' oscillatory nature. The idle animation ensures a steady yet subtle movement, while hover introduces dynamic changes with a slight rotational effect to mimic air currents around an audio wave. Click effects include vibration for tactile feedback and visual ripple for added immersion.
